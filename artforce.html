About Art Force

Case Study: Subscription Program


Art Force's SmartArt rotating art program allows clients to host a program of original fine art and prints that changes every six months. To help attract clients to our program, I created a landing page that would work with our inbound marketing efforts to bring in new clients for the program.

Initial Landing Page

After a year of promoting this page, we found two key issues:

1. We no longer had enough original artwork to continue bringing in new clients to our subscription program

2. Clients interested in the subscription artwork were either interested in the program for the financial flexibility of monthly payments, or the ability to host a rotating program of original artwork.


s


Dollar Fine Art Product Launch

In order to reach a wider audience of clients, we launched Dollar Fine Art to focus on those who most valued affordability. This program includes print reproductions and allows our clients to pay monthly subscription fees until they own their artwork in under 12 month.

Home Page



Establishing Credibility

When launching a new B2B product, showcasing past projects and clients is key. To help us demonstrate the social proof necessary to build the trust of our prospects, I included a section of past client installations and testimonials.



Connecting with Artists

Our program relies on artists providing print-ready images of their artwork for us to provide to our clients. For our program to be successful we needed to not only make the site appealing to potential clients, but also entice artists to work with us



Converting Traffic

One of the main goals of the page was to integrate with digital marketing to convert traffic into leads. My design has two main avenues for lead generation. The first being standard forms and pop up offers, and the second being the package selection tool. This tool allows users to customize a package and estimate their monthly payments for the program. At the end of the form, they are then connected with an Art Consultant to help them finalize their project.
