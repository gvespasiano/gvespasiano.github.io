MileStones - Low Fidelity Testing Script



Account Setup

Welcome Statement:Thank you for participating in my usability test. In this test you will be helping me assess the usability of an application I am designing. Anything you comment is a reflection on the app, not your abilities. There are no right or wrong answers here and I’m not testing you - I’m testing how intuitive and useful my application design is!

The product you’ll be testing is called MileStones. MileStones is a decentralized social media platform that allows you to safely and conveniently share moments. These moments are securely stored and shared only with a group of other people that you select. For this test I’d like you to assume that you are an expecting parent that would like to use our service. You’ll be setting up an account on the website, creating your page, adding subscribers and then posting your content. As you move through the tasks, please think aloud. I’m interested in your thought process and how you understand what you see and think you can do on each page.

Task 1. Begin setting up your account.

  • What information are you used to seeing at a welcome page?
  • Can you think of any ways that this page could make signing up easier?


Task 2. Set up the fields, please think aloud as you look at the possible data entries

  • What do you think is clickable on this page?
  • What do you think clicking “add child” will do?
  • Can you think of any other information you’d like to use to set up the family members who might be involved in managing the account?


Task 3. Complete the setup on this page

  • Given the limited information on the page, what do you think the first text entry area is setting up?
  • What might the “pending” mean?
  • [After entering the url] why do you think it says “available”?
  • You have already entered a password. What do you think this password here is for? [Explain password if needed]
  • As we’ve seen, the black lines are used to represent text. What are the details you would need to understand the function of this password?


Task 4. Invite someone to view your page

  • In order to invite a person, what information is required? What is optional?
  • Do you think you could use a phone number instead of an email?
  • Upon inviting your first person, what do you expect to happen to the screen? Let’s assume you’d like to invite multiple people. How might you keep track of this?
  • Given the information you’ve entered, what information and features do you think will be sent to the person you’ve invited?


Task 5. Create your page

  • What do you think the first text area does? What might you enter here if you were creating a page for your family?
  • Can you think of any other information you’d like to have displayed on a page for your family photos
  • If after setting up your account you’d like to change any of this - how would you expect to be able to do this?


Task 6. Add your first milestone

  • Given the information displayed on this page, what do you think the first step in getting started is?
  • What do you think clicking on get started will do? Where will it take you and what might you be able to do there?
